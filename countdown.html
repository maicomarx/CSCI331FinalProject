Waiting in Hyperscript

By Mico Monks

Another thing we can do in Hyperscript is waiting. Here we have a button that disappears after it's clicked. It will then give a countdown over the course of 3 seconds. After which, the button will appear again.

This can be useful to avoid spamming buttons, or for verification purposes, or even for asynchronous coding.
